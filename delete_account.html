Delete Account Instructions

Important Notice Account deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, all your transactions, budgets, goals, and personal settings will be purged from our systems and cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly through the SpendPeel app by following these steps:

1
Open SpendPeel

Launch the app on your mobile device and ensure you are logged in to the account you wish to delete.

2
Access Settings

Tap the Settings (icon) in the top header section of your main dashboard.

3
Navigate to Account

Find the "Profile" or "Account" section within the settings menu.

4
Request Deletion

Tap the "Delete Account" button at the bottom of the page.

5
Confirm Action

Read the confirmation prompt carefully and confirm that you wish to permanently remove your data.
