Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Welcome to SpendPeel. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by SpendPeel and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials through Google Firebase.
  • Financial Data: We store the transactions, budgets, and loans you enter into the app to provide you with insights and tracking capabilities.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-identifying information about the device you use to access the app for performance monitoring and crash reporting.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to maintain, protect, and improve our services, including:

  • Providing your personalized financial dashboard and progress tracking.
  • Authenticating your access to the app across multiple devices.
  • Sending important notifications or alerts regarding your spending limits.
  • Improving app performance and fixing technical issues.

3. Data Storage and Security

SpendPeel uses industry-standard cloud services, specifically Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), to store and secure your data. While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like Google Firebase for authentication, database management, and remote configuration. These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. Your Choices

You can view, edit, or delete your data at any time through the app. If you choose to delete your account, we will purge all associated personal data from our active databases in accordance with our data retention policies.

6. Links to Other Sites

Our app may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us; therefore, we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites.
